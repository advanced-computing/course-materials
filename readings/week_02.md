## One-time setup

Please do the following:

- [Add your name pronunciation and pronouns](https://courseworks2.columbia.edu/courses/233896/external_tools/62951)
- Add your profile photo in Ed, the discussion tool
  1.  [Open Ed](https://courseworks2.columbia.edu/courses/233896/external_tools/37606?display=borderless)
  1.  Click the icon in the top right
  1.  Click Settings
  1.  Add your photo
- Open [CourseWorks notification settings](https://courseworks2.columbia.edu/profile/communication) and enable the following:
  - Grading
  - Submission Comment
- Fill out [your GitHub profile](https://github.com/settings/profile) - name, avatar, etc.
